Monitor Anything with ZABBIX

You benefit from a complete range of services to meet your needs in terms of monitoring, management and follow-up of your IT infrastructures. Here are some of the areas where I can bring you my expertise.

Network monitoring

You can use Zabbix to monitor network traffic, network interfaces, and errors on network equipment.

Configure alerts for network failures or traffic thresholds exceeded.

Server monitoring

I can configure Zabbix to monitor the performance of your servers, including CPU usage, memory usage, disk space usage, and running services.

Set up alerts to be informed if defined performance thresholds are exceeded.

Private Cloud Monitoring

Monitor the performance of virtualization environments (VMware, Hyper-V, etc.) by collecting metrics such as CPU usage, allocated memory, and VM movements.

Service monitoring

Zabbix allows you to monitor various essential services, such as Active Directory, Exchange Server, Apache servers, databases, network services, and virtualization environments.

Application Monitoring

Integrate Zabbix with business applications to monitor performance and availability of critical applications.

Create custom dashboards to display key metrics.

By offering these services, you will help strengthen the monitoring and management of your entire IT environment.
This approach will provide your company with a centralized, reliable and secure monitoring system, facilitating the early detection of problems, optimization of performance and the implementation of proactive management strategies to maintain the stability of your infrastructure.
