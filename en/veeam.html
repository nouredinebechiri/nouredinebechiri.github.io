Ensure the availability of your data with Veeam

As an experienced engineer with Veeam Backup and Replication, I can offer you a full range of services to meet your backup, replication and virtual data management needs. Here are some of the services I can help you with

Veeam Architecture Design

I can help you design the overall architecture of the Veeam platform based on the specific needs of the organization. This involves determining the number of backup servers, the backup network topology, the storage strategy, etc.

Installing and configuring Veeam Backup & Replication

I can install and configure Veeam Backup & Replication on backup servers. This includes setting up backup and replication roles, configuring backup destinations, etc.

Data Backup and Restore

I will configure backup policies for servers, virtual machines, and critical data. I will ensure that data is backed up regularly and restore tests are performed to ensure the integrity of the backups.

Data replication

I will set up data replication to enable fast and efficient disaster recovery. This involves configuring destination sites, replication times, etc.

Integration with storage systems

I will integrate Veeam with existing storage systems to benefit from features such as deduplication, compression and snapshot support.

Scheduling Backups

I will help you schedule backups to minimize impact on system performance and ensure data availability when needed.

By providing these services, you will help the organization implement a robust backup and disaster recovery solution, thereby effectively protecting data, minimizing losses in the event of an incident and ensuring business continuity.
