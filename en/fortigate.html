Secure your infrastructure with FortiGate

I can guide you in designing and deploying your FortiGate-based security infrastructure based on your protection, performance and redundancy requirements. This includes choosing the right FortiGate firewall model based on network traffic, number of users and required features (SSL Inspection, IPS, Sandboxing, etc.).

Installation & configuration

I can install and configure your FortiGate firewall (network interfaces, routes, DNS, NTP, etc.). Setting up security policies adapted to your organization. Configuring advanced features such as IPS, Antivirus, Web Filtering and Application Control. Integrating with other equipment such as FortiAnalyzer, FortiManager or FortiMail for centralized management.

Network traffic optimization

I can help you optimize network traffic management by configuring static and dynamic routing (OSPF, BGP). Site-to-site VPNs (IPSec, SSL) for secure remote access. Quality of Service (QoS) management to prioritize certain types of traffic. Network segmentation via VLANs.

Security & Compliance

I can configure and optimize user authentication with LDAP, RADIUS or SSO. Advanced threat protection strategies (IPS, Antivirus, FortiSandbox Sandbox). Web and application filtering to control user access. Logging and monitoring via FortiAnalyzer for better visibility and compliance with security standards.

High Availability & Backup

I can help you ensure the availability of your infrastructure by implementing, An HA (High Availability) architecture in Active/Active or Active/Passive mode. Automatic configuration backups for rapid recovery in the event of a failure. Disaster recovery plans (DRP) including redundant configurations and failover procedures.

Upgrade & Migration

If you want to update your FortiGate equipment or migrate from another firewall solution, I can assist you in, Impact analysis and migration planning. Updating firmware while minimizing service interruptions. Migrating configurations and security rules while optimizing their effectiveness.

FortiGate provides your organization with a robust, high-performance, and easy-to-manage network security solution that delivers advanced protection against cyber threats, precise traffic control, and unified security policy management.
